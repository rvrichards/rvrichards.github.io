About

How did this all happen?

  • Late 2013

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Logged on to the internet looking for someone to partner with. There were many people to choose from but one person stood out, an Elena from France.

  • June 2014

    Problems in Paradise

    Was ready to fly out to France to begin the business, then ran into some technical issues, so had to postpone, could this be the end?

  • October 2014

    An Autumn in Paris

    Finally things were arranged, tickets booked and accomodations set. The flight from Vancouver to Iceland to Paris went without problems. Time to begin the face to face collaboration.

  • January 2015

    Visa Problems

    The collaboration was moving along successfully, but then there were visa issues, no worries, back to Canada and filling out tons of paperwork for a years visa, and a few months later was back in Bordeaux. Ready to Rock-n-roll

  • September 2015

    Erloeb Industries Created

    After careful planning, moving to a bigger office and production centre, we are close to the official launch. Things are moving faster now...
